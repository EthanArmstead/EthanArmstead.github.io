Symptoms and Warning Signs

In the early stages symptoms for TB can look very similar to those of the common cold or the flu, although if it becomes severe the symptoms will become distinct. Symptoms include:

  • Chronic or persistent cough and sputum production, if advanced enough sputum may contain blood.
  • Fatigue
  • Lack of Appetite
  • Weight loss
  • Fever
  • Night Sweats
